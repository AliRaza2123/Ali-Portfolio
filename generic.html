A little about me!

As a recent graduate with a Master's degree in Data Science and Analytics from the University of Calgary. My academic journey began with an undergraduate degree in Economics. It was here, through my econometrics courses, that I developed a keen interest in data analysis and modeling. My master's program further honed this passion, equipping me with a diverse skill set in tools such as SQL, Python, R, and data visualization with Power BI and Tableau.

Beyond my academic pursuits, sports have played a vital role in my life. I was a basketball player for the University of Calgary, and this experience has seamlessly integrated into my data analytics career, providing unique insights and perspectives. Additionally, I continue to contribute to my local community by coaching basketball camps, blending my love for sports with my professional life in data analytics.
